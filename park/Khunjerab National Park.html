Khunjerab National Park

Hingol National Park, Pakistan's largest, is a stunning blend of desert, wetlands, and mountains along the Makran coast in Balochistan. It's home to diverse wildlife, unique landmarks like the "Princess of Hope" rock formation, mud volcanoes, and the sacred Hinglaj Mata Temple. This park is a haven for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike!
